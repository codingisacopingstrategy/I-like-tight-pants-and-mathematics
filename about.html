About

I like tight pants and mathematics aims to motivate designers and artists to get more involved in the world of computer programmers.

To this end, Eric Schrijver edits a small group of writers, each with their own interest area:

  • baseline (graphic design)
  • jenseits (religion)
  • tellyou (teaching)
  • habitus (critical theory)
  • glitt (fashion)
  • bnf (computer programming).

I like tight pants and mathematics has been launched July 30th, during the Summernest exhibition in The Hague. New entries will appear every Monday and Thursday, at 22:00 CET (you can follow the RSS).

The art direction, by You & McCuskey, is made possible through a grant by Stroom Den Haag.
